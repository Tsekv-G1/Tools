Алхам 1
Анхны холбоо барих
💡 Alibaba дээр supplier-н "Contact Supplier" товч дарж энэ мессежийг илгээнэ. Мессежийн эхэнд барааны нэрийг орлуулаарай.
Hello, I found your product on Alibaba and I am interested in placing an order. Could you please provide the following information: 1. Product price per unit (MOQ and bulk price) 2. Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ) 3. Available colors and sizes 4. Sample availability and sample cost 5. Production lead time 6. Shipping options to Mongolia (UB) I am a reseller based in Mongolia and plan to order regularly if the quality meets our requirements. Looking forward to your reply. Best regards
Алхам 2
Sample захиалах
💡 Хариу ирсний дараа sample захиалахад ашиглана. Ихэвчлэн нийт бараа + хүргэлтийн зардлыг урьдчилж төлнө.
Hello, Thank you for your quick response. I would like to order 1–2 samples to test the product quality before placing a bulk order. Could you please confirm: 1. Sample price (including shipping to Mongolia/Ulaanbaatar) 2. Shipping method and estimated delivery time 3. Payment method for sample order (PayPal / Alibaba Trade Assurance) If the sample quality is good, I plan to place a larger order of [XX] units. Please send me a proforma invoice for the sample. Thank you!
Алхам 3
MOQ багасгах хүсэлт
💡 MOQ хэт өндөр байвал энэ мессежийг ашиглана. Ихэнх supplier нь жижиг захиалгад зөвшөөрдөг — шаардахаас бүү ай.
Hello, I am very interested in your product, but the MOQ of [XXX] units is too high for my first order. As a new customer testing the Mongolian market, I would like to start with a smaller quantity — around [XX–XX] units — to verify demand before scaling up. If the first order goes well, I am committed to placing larger orders every [30–60] days. Could you please make an exception and allow a smaller trial order this time? I am ready to proceed with payment immediately if we can agree on this. Thank you for your understanding!
Алхам 4
Үнэ тохиролцох
💡 Alibaba дээрх үнэ ихэвчлэн тохиролцох боломжтой. 10–20% хямдрал хүсэх нь боломжтой — ялангуяа дахин дахин захиалах амлалт өгвэл.
Hello, Thank you for your quote. Your product looks great. I have received quotes from a few other suppliers for similar products at a lower price. However, I prefer to work with your company based on your reviews and product quality. Could you offer a better price for an order of [XX] units? I am looking for a price around $[X.XX] per unit. If we can agree on pricing, I am ready to place the order today and can become a long-term repeat customer. Please let me know your best price. Best regards
Алхам 5
Захиалга баталгаажуулах
💡 Үнэ, тоо хэмжээ тохирсны дараа ашиглана. Trade Assurance ашиглах нь хамгийн найдвартай.
Hello, We have agreed on the following order details. Please confirm: - Product: [PRODUCT NAME] - Quantity: [XX] units - Unit price: $[X.XX] - Total: $[XXX] - Color/Size/Spec: [SPECIFY] - Shipping method: [Air / Sea / Express] - Shipping to: Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia Payment method: Alibaba Trade Assurance (preferred for buyer protection) Please send me a Trade Assurance order link so I can proceed with payment. After payment, please share: 1. Order confirmation 2. Tracking number once shipped 3. Packing list / invoice Thank you! I look forward to a long-term business relationship.
